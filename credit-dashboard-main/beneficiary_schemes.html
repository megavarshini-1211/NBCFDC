Explore Our Schemes

Find the right financial and skill support for your needs.

General Loan Scheme

Concessional loans for various income-generating activities.

  • Max. Loan: Up to ₹15 lakh
  • Eligibility: Family income ≤ ₹3 lakh p.a.
  • Interest: 6% to 8%
  • Repayment: Up to 8 years

Micro Finance Scheme

Small loans for tiny enterprises, with a focus on women entrepreneurs.

  • Max. Loan: Up to ₹60,000
  • Focus: Mahila Samriddhi Yojana
  • Funding: NBCFDC funds 90–95%
  • Repayment: Up to 4 years

Skill Development (SDTP)

Short-term skill training, upskilling, and reskilling programs.

  • Target Group: Backward Classes, EBCs, etc.
  • Standards: NSQF Compliant
  • Financial Aid: Stipend of ₹1,000/month

Vocational Training Loan (VETLS)

Loans for vocational training courses from 6 months to 2 years.

  • Max. Loan: Up to ₹1.5 lakh
  • Eligibility: Family income ≤ ₹3 lakh p.a.
  • Interest: 4% p.a. (with rebate for women)
  • Repayment: Up to 7 years

Technology Upgradation Scheme

Financial aid for technology, tools, and infrastructure in enterprise clusters.

  • Objective: Improve productivity & quality
  • Funding: 90–100% financial assistance
