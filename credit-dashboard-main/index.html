A Catalyst for Social and Economic Growth

NBCFDC provides financial assistance for economic and developmental activities for the benefit of Backward Classes, ensuring equitable growth and self-employment opportunities across the nation.

  • ✓ Concessional Loans: Affordable credit for diverse enterprises.
  • ✓ Skill Development: Vocational training to enhance employability.
  • ✓ Digital Empowerment: Streamlined, transparent online service delivery.
